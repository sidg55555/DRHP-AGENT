## INCREASE IN AUTHORISED SHARE CAPITAL IN PREPARATION FOR INITIAL PUBLIC OFFERING & ALTERATION IN THE CAPITAL CLAUSE OF MEMORANDUM OF ASSOCIATION

1. "RESOLVED THAT pursuant to the provisions of Section 61 and 64 and other applicable provisions, if any, of the Companies Act, 2013 (including any amendment thereto or re-enactment thereof) and the rules framed thereunder, the consent of the Board of Directors of the Company be and is hereby accorded, subject to the approval of shareholders in the Annual General Meeting to be held on 29th September 2025, to increase the Authorized Share Capital of the Company from existing Rs. 3,00,000/- (Rupees Three Lakh Only) divided into 20,000 (Twenty Thousand) Equity Shares of Rs. 10/- each and 10,000 (Ten Thousand) Compulsorily Convertible Preference Shares of Rs. 10/- each, to Rs. 11,00,00,000/- (Rupees Eleven Crore Only) divided into 1,09,90,000 (One Crore Nine Lakh Ninety Thousand) Equity Shares of Rs. 10/- each and 10,000 (Ten Thousand) Compulsorily Convertible Preference Shares of Rs. 10/- each, by creation of 1,09,70,000 (One Crore Nine Lakh Seventy Thousand) additional Equity Shares of Rs. 10/- each ranking pari passu with the existing equity shares.

2. RESOLVED FURTHER THAT pursuant to the provisions of Section 13, 61 and 64 and other applicable provisions of the Companies Act, 2013 (including any amendment thereto or re-enactment thereof) and the rules framed thereunder, the consent of the Board of Directors of the Company be and is hereby accorded, subject to the approval of shareholders in the Annual General Meeting to be held on 29th September 2025, for substituting Clause V of the Memorandum of Association of the Company with the following clause:

V. The Authorised Share Capital of the Company is Rs. 11,00,00,000/- (Rupees Eleven Crore Only) divided into 1,09,90,000 (One Crore Nine Lakh Ninety Thousand) Equity Shares of Rs. 10/- (Rupees Ten) each and 10,000 (Ten Thousand) Compulsorily Convertible Preference Shares of Rs. 10/- (Rupees Ten) each.

3. FURTHER RESOLVED THAT the above increase in authorised share capital is being undertaken in preparation for the Company's proposed Initial Public Offering (IPO) and listing on the stock exchanges, and is in accordance with the advice of the Company's investment bankers and legal advisors.

4. FURTHER RESOLVED THAT Mr. Arjun Mehta, Director of the Company be and is hereby authorized to sign and file the necessary documents with the concerned authorities including Registrar of Companies, Maharashtra, SEBI, BSE, NSE and any other regulatory authority, and to do all such acts, deeds and things as may be necessary or expedient in connection with the proposed IPO."
