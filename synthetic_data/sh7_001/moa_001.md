# THE COMPANIES ACT, 2013

# MEMORANDUM OF ASSOCIATION

# TECHNOVA PRIVATE LIMITED

**I.** The Name of the Company is **Technova Private Limited**.

**II.** The Registered Office of the Company will be situated in the state of Maharashtra under the jurisdiction of ROC Mumbai.

**III.** The objects for which the Company is established are:

**(A) THE MAIN OBJECTS TO BE PURSUED BY THE COMPANY ON ITS INCORPORATION ARE:**

1. To carry on the business of developing, designing, deploying, selling, licensing, and supporting software products, technology solutions, artificial intelligence tools, and digital platforms for use by financial institutions, capital markets participants, investment banks, compliance teams, and related entities in India and abroad.

2. To provide consulting, advisory, and professional services in the areas of financial technology, regulatory technology (RegTech), data analytics, machine learning, and automation to banks, non-banking financial companies, brokerage firms, asset management companies, and other financial sector entities.

3. To establish, operate, and manage technology infrastructure including cloud computing, data centres, application programming interfaces (APIs), and software-as-a-service (SaaS) platforms for the financial services industry.

4. To enter into joint ventures, collaborations, or strategic partnerships with companies, institutions, and entities in India or abroad for the purpose of developing and commercialising technology products and services.

**(B) MATTERS WHICH ARE NECESSARY FOR FURTHERANCE OF THE OBJECTS SPECIFIED IN CLAUSE 3(A) ARE:**

1. To purchase, lease, or otherwise acquire any movable or immovable property, intellectual property rights, patents, trade marks, or licenses which the Company may deem necessary or convenient for the purpose of its main business.

2. To recruit, train, and employ technology professionals, data scientists, financial analysts, legal experts, and other personnel necessary for carrying on the business of the Company.

3. To borrow or raise money, issue debentures, bonds, or other instruments, and to mortgage or hypothecate the assets of the Company for the purpose of its business, subject to the provisions of the Companies Act, 2013.

4. To invest the funds of the Company in such manner and on such terms as may be determined by the Board of Directors from time to time, subject to the applicable laws.

5. To enter into any arrangements with Government, regulatory authorities, banks, or other entities that may seem conducive to the main objects of the Company.

6. To do all such other things as are incidental or conducive to the attainment of the above objects or any of them.

**IV.** The Liability of the members is Limited. This liability is limited to the amount unpaid, if any, on the shares held by them.

**V.** The Authorised Share Capital of the Company is Rs. 1,00,000/- (Rupees One Lakh Only) divided into 10,000 (Ten Thousand) Equity Shares of Rs. 10/- (Rupees Ten) each.
