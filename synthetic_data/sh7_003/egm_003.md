## TO AMEND THE MEMORANDUM OF ASSOCIATION & INCREASE IN AUTHORISED SHARE CAPITAL BY CREATION OF PREFERENCE SHARES

### SPECIAL BUSINESS

To consider, and if thought fit, to pass with or without modification(s), the following resolution as a Special Resolution:

1. "RESOLVED THAT pursuant to the provisions of Section 13, 61 and 64 and other applicable provisions of the Companies Act, 2013 (including any amendment thereto or re-enactment thereof) and the rules framed thereunder, the consent of the members of the Company be and is hereby accorded for substituting Clause V of the Memorandum of Association of the Company with the following clause:

V. The Authorised Share Capital of the Company is Rs. 3,00,000/- (Rupees Three Lakh Only) divided into 20,000 (Twenty Thousand) Equity Shares of face value of Rs. 10/- (Rupees Ten) each and 10,000 (Ten Thousand) Compulsorily Convertible Preference Shares of face value of Rs. 10/- (Rupees Ten) each."

2. "RESOLVED FURTHER THAT pursuant to the provisions of Section 61 read with Section 64 and other applicable provisions, if any, of the Companies Act, 2013 (including any amendment thereto or re-enactment thereof) and the rules framed thereunder, the consent of the members of the Company be and is hereby accorded to increase the Authorized Share Capital of the Company from existing Rs. 2,00,000/- (Rupees Two Lakh Only) divided into 20,000 (Twenty Thousand) Equity Shares of Rs. 10/- each, to Rs. 3,00,000/- (Rupees Three Lakh Only) divided into 20,000 (Twenty Thousand) Equity Shares of Rs. 10/- each and 10,000 (Ten Thousand) Compulsorily Convertible Preference Shares of Rs. 10/- each.

RESOLVED FURTHER THAT the Memorandum of Association of the Company be altered accordingly."

3. RESOLVED FURTHER THAT for the purpose of giving effect to this resolution, the Board of Directors of the Company be and is hereby authorized to take all such steps and actions and give such directions as may be in its absolute discretion deemed necessary.

## CERTIFIED TRUE COPY OF THE EXPLANATORY STATEMENT FOR ITEM NO. 1 AND 2

### TO INCREASE THE AUTHORISED CAPITAL AND CREATE A NEW CLASS OF PREFERENCE SHARES

The Company has been in discussions with a Series B investor who requires the issuance of Compulsorily Convertible Preference Shares (CCPS). The existing authorised capital of Rs. 2,00,000/- comprising only equity shares is insufficient to accommodate the proposed CCPS issuance. It is therefore necessary to increase the authorised capital to Rs. 3,00,000/- by creating a new class of 10,000 CCPS of Rs. 10/- each.

### TO AMEND THE MEMORANDUM OF ASSOCIATION

The capital clause (Clause V) of the Memorandum of Association must be amended to reflect the new capital structure including both equity shares and the newly created preference share class.

None of the Directors are in any way concerned or interested in the said resolution except to the extent of their shareholdings in the Company.
