## INCREASE IN AUTHORISED SHARE CAPITAL BY CREATION OF PREFERENCE SHARES & ALTERATION IN THE CAPITAL CLAUSE OF MEMORANDUM OF ASSOCIATION

1. "RESOLVED THAT pursuant to the provisions of Section 61 and 64 and other applicable provisions, if any, of the Companies Act, 2013 (including any amendment thereto or re-enactment thereof) and the rules framed thereunder, the consent of the Board of Directors of the Company be and is hereby accorded, subject to the approval of shareholders in the General Meeting, to increase the Authorized Share Capital of the Company from existing Rs. 2,00,000/- (Rupees Two Lakh Only) divided into 20,000 (Twenty Thousand) Equity Shares of Rs. 10/- each to Rs. 3,00,000/- (Rupees Three Lakh Only) by creation of 10,000 (Ten Thousand) Compulsorily Convertible Preference Shares (CCPS) of Rs. 10/- (Rupees Ten) each.

2. RESOLVED FURTHER THAT pursuant to the provisions of Section 13, 61 and 64 and other applicable provisions of the Companies Act, 2013 (including any amendment thereto or re-enactment thereof) and the rules framed thereunder, the consent of the Board of Directors of the Company be and is hereby accorded, subject to the approval of shareholders in the General Meeting, for substituting Clause V of the Memorandum of Association of the Company with the following clause:

V. The Authorised Share Capital of the Company is Rs. 3,00,000/- (Rupees Three Lakh Only) divided into 20,000 (Twenty Thousand) Equity Shares of Rs. 10/- (Rupees Ten) each and 10,000 (Ten Thousand) Compulsorily Convertible Preference Shares of Rs. 10/- (Rupees Ten) each.

3. FURTHER RESOLVED THAT an Extra Ordinary General Meeting of the members of the Company be convened on 22nd July 2021 at 11:00 A.M. at the registered office of the Company to seek approval of the members for the aforesaid increase.

4. FURTHER RESOLVED THAT Mr. Arjun Mehta, Director of the Company be and is hereby authorized to sign and file the necessary documents with the concerned authorities including Registrar of Companies, Maharashtra."
